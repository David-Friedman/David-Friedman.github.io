Men Without Hats

  • Description
  • Links

Amid the chaos of one of the busiest semesters of my school career, I was determined. Determined to make a campy B-movie punk film with no absolutely no budget.

What was supposed to be quaint five-minute film for my Intro to Film class turned into a rambunctious 20-minute spectacle about people who instantly die when they lose their hats.

Please enjoy it here.

Watch the film at this link!
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=n9uTr0WHwK8

This project was done in the timespan of about 4 weeks.
We did 10 hours worth of shooting, and I did about 4-5 hours worth of editing. Inspired by the sights and sounds of (H)Atlanta I knew exactly what I wanted, so I made it fast.

All the music, sadly, is not done by me. I would have loved to score a film, but just didn't have the time. In addition, all the scenes are choreographed with the music down to some of the word choices, so it would be difficult to replace it.

Thank you to everyone who helped me on this. I could not have made this without you all!

Derive Me Now

  • Description
  • Links

A music video I made in high school! A parody of Queen's "Don't Stop Me Now."

It's about the sinister college board taking away Math, the love of the main character's life.

We filmed this for a few hours after I took the SAT, and I almost passed out from dehydration (now I always carry around water with me). Editing took around 20 hours because I had a lot of really cheesy special effects, but I love it.

There's even a couple of homages to this video in Men Without Hats, so keep an eye out for them!

Watch it here!

Watch the video at this link!

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KJ6DzP_SmkA

The music, of course, is a parody of Queen's "Don't Stop Me Now" to the tune of Calculus.

Special thanks to Luke Dobeck and O'Bryan Moore for helping me make this, and for O'Bryan for introducing me to "Don't Stop Me Now" in fifth grade. You guys rock!
